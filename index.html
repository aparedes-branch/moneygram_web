How to send money online
Send money online with peace of mind

With MoneyGram, money transfers are reliable, fast and your loved ones can pick up at agent locations around the world or have funds deposited into their bank or mobile accounts.

1

Register or log in

Enter your information so that we can verify your identity.

2

Select a receiver or send to someone new

Tell us who you are sending to, how they want to receive the money and how much you want to send.

3

Choose how to pay and send

You can either pay with your credit1 or debit card, or directly from your bank account.

 

Never send money to someone you don't know.

Fast

With many options to choose from, its fast and easy to send money. Many transfers arrive within minutes.2

Reliable

MoneyGram online allows you to send your information and money with confidence.

Convenient

Send money anytime from anywhere. Our global network includes well-known retailers and businesses, in more than 200 countries and territories, making cash pick up easy for your receiver.
